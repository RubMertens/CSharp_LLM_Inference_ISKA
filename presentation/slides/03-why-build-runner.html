Why Build Your Own Runner?

    Control — Choose your model, tune every parameter, own the stack
  • ⚡
    Latency — No network round-trips, no queue, direct GPU access

    Cost — GPU time is cheaper than API tokens at scale
  • 🔒
    Privacy — Data never leaves your infrastructure
  • 🧠
    Understanding — You can't optimize what you don't understand
