Positional Embedding

Transformers have no built-in sense of word order

Without position info, these are identical to the model:

"the cat sat on the mat" "mat the on sat cat the"

🔄 Modern approach: RoPE (Rotary Position Embeddings)

Encodes position by rotating the embedding vector — nearby tokens get similar rotations, distant tokens get different ones. Used in LLaMA, Mistral, Qwen, and most modern models.
