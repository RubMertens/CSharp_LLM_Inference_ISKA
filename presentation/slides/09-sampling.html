Sampling Strategies

The model gives us probabilities. How do we pick?

🌡️ Temperature

The creativity dial.

Low (0.1) = predictable, safe. High (1.5) = creative, chaotic. Zero = always pick the top choice.

🎯 Top-K

Only consider the top K options.

K=50 means ignore everything except the 50 most likely tokens. Simple but effective.

📊 Top-P (Nucleus)

Keep tokens until probability sums to P.

P=0.9 means keep enough tokens to cover 90% of the probability mass. Adapts to confidence.

In practice, these are often combined: apply temperature first, then top-p, then top-k.
