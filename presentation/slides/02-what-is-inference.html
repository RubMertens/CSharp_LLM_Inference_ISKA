What is Inference?

Asking the model a question and getting an answer.

🎓 Training

Studying for the exam. The model reads billions of texts and learns patterns over weeks on hundreds of GPUs.

Expensive. Slow. Done once.

⚡ Inference

Taking the exam. The model uses what it learned to generate a response — one token at a time.

Cheap(er). Fast. Done millions of times.
