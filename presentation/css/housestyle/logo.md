# Info Support -- Logo Guidelines

> Source: Chapter 3, pages 19-21.

## The Logo

The Info Support logo consists of:
1. The **wordmark** "infoSupport" in a custom logotype
2. The **pay-off** "Solid Innovator" positioned below

These two elements are **inseparable** -- the pay-off "Solid Innovator" is never used apart from the logo.

## Colour Variants

The logo may only be used in **two colour treatments**:

1. **Full colour** -- the standard blue logo (Info Support Blauw `#00A3E0` for "info" and decorative elements; Navy `#003865` for "Support" text; "Solid Innovator" in Info Support Blauw)
2. **White (reversed)** -- for use on dark or photographic backgrounds

No other colour variations are permitted.

## Scaling

The logo must always be scaled **proportionally as a whole**. The relationship between the wordmark and the pay-off is fixed and may not be altered.

## Clear Space (Vrije marge)

- A defined whitespace zone surrounds the logo, equal to **1/16th of the document width**.
- No other visual elements may intrude into this zone.

## Logo Size

- The logo may span **3 or 4 grid blocks** wide (on the 16-column grid), depending on the required visibility.

## Placement per Audience

| Audience | Logo Position |
|---|---|
| Handelsdrukwerk en signing | Bottom-left |
| Business | Bottom-right |
| Intern | Bottom-right |
| Arbeidsmarkt | Bottom-right |

## Do's

- Use on a white background (full-colour logo)
- Use on an Info Support Blauw background (white logo)
- Use on a Navy background (white logo)
- Use on a photo with sufficient contrast (white logo, calm area of the photo)
- Use on a photo with sufficient contrast (full-colour logo on light area)

## Don'ts

- **Never** rotate or skew the logo
- **Never** change the proportions between wordmark and pay-off
- **Never** separate "Solid Innovator" from the wordmark
- **Never** use colours other than the approved blue/navy or white
- **Never** place on a busy photographic background where the logo becomes unreadable
- **Never** place the full-colour logo on a dark background that lacks contrast
