# Info Support Brand Guide -- Digest

> Source: *Huisstijlrichtlijnen Info Support*, versie februari 2020 (65 pages, Dutch-language PDF).

## Brand Promise

**"Vooruit"** (Forward) -- Info Support takes the extra step, going beyond client expectations while maintaining reliability and a solid foundation. The brand promise "Vooruit" is visualised as a forward motion at a 45-degree angle, which gives rise to the signature triangle shape used across all materials.

## Tagline

**Solid Innovator** -- always appears together with the logo wordmark; never used separately.

## Writing Convention

"Info Support" is always written as two separate words, both capitalised. Never as "InfoSupport", "info support", or "Infosupport".

## Target Audiences (Doelgroepen)

The house style distinguishes **four audiences**, each with its own sub-palette and style-element intensity:

| Audience | Dutch Name | Character |
|---|---|---|
| Trade / Signing | Handelsdrukwerk en signing | Most calm and business-like; primary colours only; style elements never overlap |
| Business | Business | Professional with a subtle code motif; max 2 secondary colours; triangles may not overlap |
| Internal | Intern | Close to Business but with slightly more freedom; elements may overlap for variety |
| Labour Market | Arbeidsmarkt | Most colourful and expressive; all secondary colours available; elements overlap freely |

## Core Visual Elements

1. **Triangles (Driehoeken)** -- the primary graphic motif. The longest edge must always point upper-right. Available in three variants: solid fill, diagonal-line fill, small-triangle-pattern fill.
2. **Fast Forward arrows (Pijltjes)** -- the >> icon, used as a style accent and for pagination.
3. **Small triangle (Klein driehoekje)** -- a miniature accent element placed freely; only in navy.
4. **Code snippet overlay** -- used in Business, Intern, and Arbeidsmarkt materials to subtly communicate "tech".

## Do's

- Always use the brand colours as specified per audience.
- Maintain the grid system (16-column for margins, 8-column for triangle positioning).
- Keep whitespace (vrije marge) of 1/16th document width on all sides.
- Triangles' long edge must point to the upper right.
- Use the Rule of Thirds or centred composition for photography.
- Seek perspective and leading lines in images.

## Don'ts

- Never rotate the logo, distort proportions, or separate "Solid Innovator" from the wordmark.
- Never place the logo on a busy photo background without sufficient contrast.
- Never let triangles point the wrong way (long edge to the left).
- Do not combine more than 2 secondary colours per item (Business/Intern); Arbeidsmarkt allows more.
- Avoid cosy, passive, old-fashioned, informal, or blurry photography.
- Never use warm-toned imagery; keep tones cool and bright.

## Print vs. Digital

- **Print** uses Pantone (PMS) + CMYK values; colour build differs between coated (Silk Mc) and uncoated (Olin Absolute White) paper stock.
- **Digital** uses HEX/RGB values.
- The guide explicitly notes that colour builds are paper-dependent (see Chapter 10 -- Materialen).
