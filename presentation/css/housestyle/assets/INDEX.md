## Notes

- All images were embedded in the PDF at 144 DPI in CMYK colour space (except a few RGB variants for screen display). The JPEG files retain their original CMYK encoding.
- The PDF's vector elements (triangles, grids, arrows, code-snippet overlays) are drawn as vector paths and were **not** captured by `pdfimages`. They are described in detail in `imagery.md` with reproducible CSS/SVG snippets.
- The logo on page 19 is a vector graphic and was not extractable as a raster image. It is described in `logo.md`.
