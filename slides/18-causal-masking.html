Causal Masking

Tokens can only look at the past, never at the future

Why?

During generation, future tokens don't exist yet. The model predicts them one by one.

How?

Attention scores above the diagonal are set to -∞. After softmax, they effectively become 0.

Causal Mask
for (int i = 0; i < seqLen; i++)
  for (int j = i + 1; j < seqLen; j++)
    scores[i, j] = float.NegativeInfinity;

Triangular pattern — simple but essential for autoregressive generation.
