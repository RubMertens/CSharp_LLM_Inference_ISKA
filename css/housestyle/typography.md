# Info Support -- Typography

> Source: Chapter 4, pages 22-28.

## Overview

The house style uses **primary** and **secondary** fonts. Secondary fonts are fallbacks used when the primary fonts are unavailable to the recipient.

---

## Primaire fonts (Primary Fonts)

### 1. Px Grotesk - Bold

- **Role:** Titles, quotes
- **Availability:** Commercial typeface (not freely available)
- **Metrics (example sizes):**

| Property | Small | Medium | Large |
|---|---|---|---|
| Corps (font size) | 12 pt | 36 pt | 72 pt |
| Interlinie (line-height) | 14 pt (+2) | 38 pt (+2) | 74 pt (+2) |
| Letterspatiering (letter-spacing) | -50 | -50 | -50 |

- Uppercase: allowed (with restriction -- only for single-line headings with a subtitle, e.g., on a poster or roll-up banner).
- Lowercase: standard use.

### 2. Px Grotesk - Screen

- **Role:** Used *in combination with* Px Grotesk - Bold. Only single vowels (a, o, u, i, e), digits, and the ampersand may be set in Px Grotesk - Screen. Two Screen characters must never appear adjacent.
- **Minimum corps:** 18 pt
- **Availability:** Commercial typeface
- **Metrics:**

| Property | Small | Medium | Large |
|---|---|---|---|
| Corps | 20 pt | 40 pt | 60 pt |
| Interlinie | 22 pt (+2) | 42 pt (+2) | 62 pt (+2) |
| Letterspatiering | -50 | -50 | -50 |

- Same uppercase restriction as Px Grotesk - Bold.

### 3. Graphik - Regular

- **Role:** Body text (leestekst)
- **Availability:** Commercial typeface by Grilli Type
- **Metrics:**

| Property | Small | Medium | Large |
|---|---|---|---|
| Corps | 12 pt | 36 pt | 72 pt |
| Interlinie | 16 pt (+4) | 40 pt (+4) | 76 pt (+4) |
| Letterspatiering | 0 | 0 | 0 |

### 4. Graphik - Bold

- **Role:** Sub-headings (tussenkopjes) and introductory text (inleidende teksten)
- **Availability:** Commercial typeface by Grilli Type
- **Metrics:**

| Property | Small | Medium | Large |
|---|---|---|---|
| Corps | 12 pt | 36 pt | 72 pt |
| Interlinie | 16 pt (+4) | 40 pt (+4) | 76 pt (+4) |
| Letterspatiering | 0 | 0 | 0 |

---

## Secundaire fonts (Secondary / Fallback Fonts)

Used when primary fonts are not available (e.g., in e-mails, letters, environments without custom fonts).

### 1. Arial - Regular

- **Replaces:** Graphik - Regular
- **Role:** Body text
- **Metrics:**

| Property | Small | Medium | Large |
|---|---|---|---|
| Corps | 10 pt | 36 pt | 72 pt |
| Interlinie | 14 pt (+4) | 40 pt (+4) | 76 pt (+4) |
| Letterspatiering | 0 | 0 | 0 |

### 2. Arial - Bold

- **Replaces:** Px Grotesk - Bold (for titles) and Graphik - Bold (for sub-headings)
- **Role:** Titles, quotes, sub-headings
- **Metrics:**

| Property | Small | Medium | Large |
|---|---|---|---|
| Corps | 12 pt | 36 pt | 72 pt |
| Interlinie | 14 pt (+2) | 38 pt (+2) | 74 pt (+2) |
| Letterspatiering | 0 | 0 | 0 |

---

## Typographic Hierarchy (recommended for digital/presentations)

| Level | Font | Weight | Suggested Size |
|---|---|---|---|
| H1 (Main title) | Px Grotesk Bold (or Arial Bold) | Bold | 36-72 pt |
| H2 (Sub-heading) | Graphik Bold (or Arial Bold) | Bold | 18-36 pt |
| H3 (Section head) | Graphik Bold (or Arial Bold) | Bold | 14-18 pt |
| Body | Graphik Regular (or Arial Regular) | Regular | 10-12 pt |
| Small / Caption | Graphik Regular (or Arial Regular) | Regular | 8-10 pt |

> **Note:** The guide does not provide an explicit H1/H2/H3/body hierarchy for digital use. The table above is inferred from the example corps sizes and intended usages described in the guide.

## Key Rules

- Line-height is always **corps + 2 pt** for heading fonts (Px Grotesk, Arial Bold) and **corps + 4 pt** for body fonts (Graphik, Arial Regular).
- Letter-spacing is **-50** for Px Grotesk fonts and **0** for Graphik and Arial.
- Uppercase is restricted to single-line headings with a subtitle on large-format items only.
- For internal communications (e-mails, letters) in Arial, black text colour may also be used.
